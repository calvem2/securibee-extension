the buzz about 'strong' passwords

what's a 'strong' password?

A strong password can help you minimize the likelihood of an attacker learning your password.

more tips for strong passwords

  • try to intersperse numbers and symbols instead of sticking them at the end
  • don't include personal information that can be easily guessed or obtained through social media (e.g. names, dates)
  • use a unique password for each online account

why you should care

Your online accounts often have personal information that you don't want anyone else to access.

Strong password security can help you avoid the consequences of someone hacking your account.

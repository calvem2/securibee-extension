the buzz about 'strong' passwords

what's a 'strong' password?

A strong password can help you minimize the likelihood of an attacker learning your password. The best, or strongest, passwords are long and hard to guess!

more tips for strong passwords

  • don't reuse passwords for multiple accounts
  • don't just stick a number/symbol at the end
  • don't use personal information (e.g. names, dates, places, hobbies)

why you should care

Your online accounts often have personal information that you don't want anyone else to access.

Strong password security can help you avoid the consequences of someone hacking your account.
